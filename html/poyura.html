    Design Challenge

    Poyura is a company that sells stationery with my art on it. It needed a webshop that represents that idea.

    Solution

    I did research about what Poyura stands for. Poyura is a brand that wants to promote positivity, self-love, and eco-friendly products. After I have created branding, I made a user-friendly webshop using WordPress.

    Home page

    Home page

    When the user visits the website, the first thing they will see is an image of a new product with the logan. This way the user has a feeling about what this website is about. When they scroll down, they will see products shown in categories. There are four columns with products and fill the screen.

    Categories

    The categories are divided by the kind of products that Poyura sells and relevant filters like new and sale. This gives the user an idea about what Poyura sells.
