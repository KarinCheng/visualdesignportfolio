    Metadata

    Project time: 6 months

    Client: School

    Tools: Sketch & Principle

    Product: UI

    Design Challenge

    How do we motivate students between 18 and 25 who are interested to improve global pollution to reduce their carbon dioxide footprint without having them feel like their quality of life is getting worse?

    Solution

    Global pollution is a big problem where a lot of different aspects play a role. So we focus on local seasonal fruits and vegetables. Our target group has to be more aware of their choices and it can inspire the wrongdoers to do the same.

    Onboarding

    Upload your recipes

    Inspire other young cooks to cook with seasonal fruits and vegetables. There are five steps to create your recipe. On every screen, you can only take one action. So you can’t get confused. At every step, you can see a preview of how the recipe would look like if you upload it. On the top of the screen, there is a bar that tracks your process. At the end of step five, it will show you a preview for the last time and then you can upload it. Your recipe will show in the feed of other users and your profile.

    Style Guide

    Recipes

    You can save recipes you want to try later. You create your cookbook. Other users won’t see the recipes you have saved. The recipe will show you how much time it takes to cook it, the ingredients, the steps, and suggestions.

    Recipes

    Simplified View

    Simplified view

    There is a button on the recipe screen that will show you a simplified view of the recipe. This feature is meant for when the user is cooking in the kitchen and need to quickly see what they need to do. Every screen explains one step. This way it won’t confuse the user and they can focus on their cooking.

    Social platform

    Season is a social platform that inspires people to cook with seasonal fruits and vegetables. Every season there are new ingredients to cook with. You share your recipes or save recipes from other users. You can rate the recipes of other users. When a season is over, the users with the best recipes will get a batch on their profile. This way creators get motivated to come up with more and better recipes.

    Social Platform

    Shopping List

    Shopping list

    You can add the ingredients of a recipe to your shopping list. You can use this list when you are in the grocery store and check them off if you have found them. It also tells you how much you need for every item. After you have got everything or don’t need the shopping list anymore, you can delete it.
